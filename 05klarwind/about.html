STORY

Klarwind was launched by a passionate business owner in Korea who has been with various social activities for disabled people in respiratory problems. Klarwind was originated from many concerns of creating cleaner air for those people. He concentrated on developing air-cleaning technology to relieve the pain of people who suffering from respiratory diseases and allergies. The brand is aiming for the best value in terms of performance, design and reliability.

Also, it won several awards at prestigious design competitions in Europe and recognized for its excellence in design. It is a leading global premium brand that develops healthcare products.

Klarwind promises to lead global air purifier market and try to stay in company with customers in their daily life with the best products & services.

    • TECHNOLOGIES

      Based on the excellent technology of air conditioning systems essential for state-of-the-art buildings in domestic and abroad, we developed air purifiers to protect people, pets, and children in danger of global air pollution.

      We also design every product with HEPA Class air filters which makes a great performance to improve air quality in your place. It removes more than 99.9% of dust, fine dust and harmful gases through 5 steps.

      In addition, we are growing as a leading company based on our long-standing know-how in eco-friendly and high-efficiency air conditioning technologies that can maintain cooling and heating at high speeds of more than 218miles per hour.
