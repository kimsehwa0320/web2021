CERTIFICATIONS & AWARDS

INTERTEK

Intertek is a Total Quality Assurance provider to industries worldwide.
It means that the minimum conditions of product safety standards are satisfied.

AHAM

AHAM’s standard for measuring an air cleaner’s efficacy is called the Clean Air Delivery Rate (CADR).
CADR indicates the volume of filtered air an air cleaner delivers, with separate scores for tobacco smoke, pollen and dust.
